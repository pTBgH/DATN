🚀 HOW TO ACCESS THE FRONTENDS:
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

METHOD 1: Port Forward (Simplest)
  $ kubectl port-forward -n frontend svc/fe-candidate 3000:3000
  → Open browser: http://localhost:3000
  → Shows: Candidate Portal

  $ kubectl port-forward -n frontend svc/fe-recruiter 3001:3000
  → Open browser: http://localhost:3001
  → Shows: Recruiter Portal

METHOD 2: Ingress (Production)
  Add to /etc/hosts:
    127.0.0.1 candidate.app.local
    127.0.0.1 recruiter.app.local
  
  → Open browser: http://candidate.app.local
  → Or: http://recruiter.app.local

$ kubectl get events -n frontend --sort-by='.lastTimestamp' | tail -10
